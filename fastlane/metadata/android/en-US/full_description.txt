Home Medkit

The application allows you to easily and quickly register and store information about medications taken.

<b>Features</b>

* Automatically add the medicine by scanning the label (works only for Russian treatments).
* The ability to add medications manually.
* Update information about the medicine if it was scanned without an internet connection.
* Edit information about medicines added manually.
* Adding a picture of the medicine.
* Sorting of medicines by expiration dates.
* Grouping of medicines by categories.
* Sound notification of the need for medication.
* Display of the nearest medication schedule.
* Reminder of the expiration date of medicines.
* Export and import the database to other devices.

Using the app, you can track the expiration dates of medicines, create a schedule for their intake and receive notifications about it. It is a reliable and convenient assistant that will help you monitor your health.
